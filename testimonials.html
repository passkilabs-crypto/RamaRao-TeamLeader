Frequently Asked Questions

What is Life Insurance, and why is it important?
Life insurance provides financial protection to your family in case of an unfortunate event and helps with future savings goals like retirement or education.
What types of LIC policies do you offer?
We offer:
  • Savings & Risk Coverage Plans
  • Pension & Retirement Plans
  • Child Plans
  • Women-Centric Plans (BIM Sakhi)
How can I become an LIC agent?
Minimum graduation is required. Complete LIC training + pass IRDAI exam. Contact us for guidance.
What are the benefits of becoming an LIC agent?
Benefits include:
  • Commission + Bonuses
  • Renewal commission up to 15 years
  • Car & House loans at attractive rates
  • Monthly stipends for women under BIM Sakhi
  • Hereditary commission
